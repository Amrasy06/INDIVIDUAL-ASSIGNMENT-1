Step-by-Step Guide to Becoming a Doctor

  1. Earn a Bachelor's Degree: Start with a science-based degree like Biomedical Science or equivalent.
  2. Pass a Medical Entrance Exam: Take an exam such as the MCAT or equivalent to enter medical school.
  3. Complete Medical School: Enroll in a 5-6 year medical program including clinical rotations.
  4. Internship and Licensing: Complete a compulsory internship and pass licensing exams to practice.
  5. Specialization and Continuing Education: Choose a specialty and stay updated through training and research.
